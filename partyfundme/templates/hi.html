Mission Statement

Welcome to my passion project! I have been involved with the Music and the Bar industry for over 20 years. These industries have been affected by the 2020 covid-19 pandemic, forcing bars and music venues into debt and risking bankruptcy, and worse, having to shut their doors down for good. Live music and neighborhood bars and restaurants have been a positive outlet and My goal is to create an online ecosystem that incentivizes venues, bartenders, and artists to connect with their customers. Interface for Bartenders to post hours to keep their regulars informed of their daily/weekly shifts. Please check back for future updates!
